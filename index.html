Meet Grace


My name is Grace and I am a student at Saint Paul College. I am majoring in an Associate of Science Degree in Computer Graphics and Visualization. I am planning to graduate in 2025 and hope I can find a job afterwards. This portfolio is a showcase of my student work. I made this portfolio using HTML and CSS, both of which I am currently learning about. I have also gained experience using...

  • Graphical web builders
  • Adobe software
  • DAWs
  • A little bit of Tumult Hype and Blender
I began using Photoshop at a young age and am now interested in pursuing a career in graphic design. I have become a certified Photoshop and Illustrator user since pursuing my current major. My other hobbies include playing video games, watching YouTube videos, cartoons and anime, and occasionally reading.
